The Licorice Root

Characteristics:

According to the quality coeffiecient the licorice root admits the requirements of technical conditions of State standard 22839-88 "The licorice root. TU"

Typical properties:

Name of exponents Characteristics and Standarn
Appearance, size, structure Integral rhizomes and roots of the cylindrical form of various length, in the thickness: roots - 7- 50 mm, rhizomes - 7 - 150 mm. Roots and rhizomes are outside covered by thin pith layer. In rhizomes the core is appreciable, in roots it is not present. On a cross-section are observed numerous medullar beams which disperse from the centre and penetrate yellowish wood and strongly developed bark. Lengthways of medullar beams in roots radial cracks are often formed.
