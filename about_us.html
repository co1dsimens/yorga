About company

H.J. Yorga was registered in 2014 and entered in the State Register of an enterprise and organization in Turkmenistan. The status of the company allows it to carry out export-import and tourist operations. During our existence, we have gained vast experience, attracted many partners and specialists. A systematic approach allows us to fully take into account both the technical and economic needs of our customers.

Objective

To become the most recognized company in Turkmenistan for the supply and provision of services in the industrial sector of the country through quality and development.

Tasks
— DEVELOP THE COMPANY'S ACTIVITIES — STAY IN FOOT WITH ADVANCED TECHNOLOGIES — CONDUCT THE MAXIMUM SAFE AND ECONOMICALLY EFFECTIVE BUSINESS
